Life on the farm

A day in the field

04:30
First light check
Walk the beds, check soil moisture, spot pest pressure before the heat sets in.
06:00
Harvest window
Most crops are cut in the coolest hours. Temperature below 18°C keeps shelf life maximum.
08:00
Grading & packing
Manual sorting, compostable packaging. Everything weighed and labelled by the farmer.
10:00
Courier handoff
Refrigerated collection. Maximum 8 hours farm-to-door for city deliveries.
14:00
Soil work
Compost turning, mulching, companion planting. The real work that makes tomorrow's harvest possible.
18:00
Evening water
Slow drip irrigation where needed. Rainwater systems. Orders confirmed for next-day delivery.
